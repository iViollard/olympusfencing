About the club

Fencing is a fun, fast-paced and exciting sport. If you would like to learn fencing, looking for some extra training or want to take your fencing to the next level you've come to the right place. Olympus Fencing Club is a welcoming and friendly club and we look forward to meeting you.

There are three weapons in Fencing: foil; epee and sabre. At Olympus Fencing club we only focus on foil.

Fencing is unique as a sport as it is able to improve:

  • fitness
  • concentration
  • coordination
  • agility
  • self-discipline


Our Friendly instructors will help introduce you to the sport in a safe, fun and friendly environment.

Who are the club?

David Standen(founder and coach)

David has been involved in Fencing for over 15 years. He has competed at the highest level both Nationally and Internationally for Great Britain. David is one of our lead coaches and has been coaching for various clubs and schools in and around London. He has coached numerous children and adults at various tournaments of all levels leading to many medals. David continues to compete nationally and internationally.

Where we train

We are based at Chiswick School (Burlington Lane, Chiswick, London, W4 3UN) in the Old Gymnasium. The club runs on a Wednesday night for children from 5:30pm – 7pm and for adults from 7pm - 8:30pm.

Lessons

One to one lessons are available on request, which last between 25 and 30 minutes. The cost is £40 for non members while members enjoy a £10 discount so that each lesson costs only £30. If 5 lessons are purchased then a discount of %15 is applied.

Beginners

Our beginners courses are six weeks long and will teach you how to:

  • move
  • how to hold and use a foil
  • attack
  • defend
  • understand the rules of the game
  • understand and use tactics
Each session on the course is 1 hour long and there are sessions that cater for both adults and children. Children are welcome between 5.30pm - 7pm and adults 7pm - 8.30pm. The 6 sessions on the course cost a total of £95.
